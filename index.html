About Me

A big driver in my life is a love for learning. It's a big part of why I'm pursuing a career in software development. When you work with software there's always more to learn. This can become frustrating but it also keeps things interesting. It's also nice to be in an industry that's alway looking for, and developing more effective solutions and trying out different ideas.

While there is more than enough to learn in software to keep me busy for a lifetime, I can't help but explore ideas and tools from other industries as well. This is mostly out of curiosity, but you never know when a challenging problem might require you to integrate a solution from an unexpected source.

When I'm not actively working on a project or trying to read something or watch youtube videos to learn more about a subject, I am probably spending time with my family, running, kickboxing, or playing in VR. If you'd like to know more about me you can check out my personal website JohnShrader.com
